# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: April 12, 2026**

## Overview

Mini Mission Kids does not collect, store, or share any personal data. This policy explains what that means in practice.

## Data Collection

Mini Mission Kids collects **no data whatsoever**. We do not collect:

- Personal information (names, email addresses, phone numbers)
- Device identifiers or usage statistics
- Location data
- Crash reports or analytics
- Any other information about you or your children

## Data Storage

All data you enter in the app (children's names, tasks, avatars, completion history) is stored **exclusively on your device**. It never leaves your device and is never transmitted to any server or third party.

## Third Parties

Mini Mission Kids does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, advertising networks, or cloud services.

## Children's Privacy

Mini Mission Kids is designed for use by families with children. Because the app collects no data at all, there is nothing to protect — no information about children is ever recorded outside of your own device.

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new date at the top.

## Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue at our support page on GitHub.
